## Key Stats (2025)
- **1,325 animals** welcomed (498 dogs + 827 cats) — highest intake in history
- **98.4% Live Release Rate** (up from 97.7% in 2024)
  - Dogs: 99.4% | Cats: 97.7%
- **1,252 adoptions** (up from 1,078 in 2024) — 476 dogs + 776 cats
- **3,506 donors** supported Heartland in 2025
- **19,913 volunteer hours** contributed
- **274 foster homes** provided **22,373 days of care** (369 dogs + 503 cats)
- **653 volunteers** contributing 19,913 hours
- Average length of stay: Dogs 24 days (down from 43), Cats 46 days (down from 59)

## Mission
"Heartland Animal Shelter provides excellent care to the most vulnerable dogs and cats until we find them good homes. Through progressive programs, outreach, and collaborative partnerships, we also work to keep all companion animals with their families."

## Vision
"Heartland is building a humane community where pets and people thrive together in Chicagoland and beyond."

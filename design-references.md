# Design References

## LifeLine Animal Project (lifelineanimal.org)
- Split hero: bold handwritten-style headline on left, animal photo on right
- Top utility bar with phone, EN/ES language toggle
- Horizontal nav: PETS | HOW TO HELP | EVENTS | OUR SERVICES | ABOUT
- Red DONATE button in nav (heart icon)
- Pet carousel: large editorial photos, "Bring Home Your Best Friend" heading
- Upcoming Events section: list format with date, time, location
- Latest Updates: news card grid
- Newsletter signup in footer

## Greenville Humane Society (greenvillehumane.com)
- Clean minimal nav with secondary utility bar
- Full-width hero with overlay text
- Quick donation amount buttons ($25, $50, $250, Other) in sticky bar
- Pet finder widget with dropdowns (species, gender, age) + "Fetch" button
- Impact stats: large numbers with labels, "Add to the Impact" CTA
- Values section: icons + one-word values
- Popup on load: event announcement with animal photo
- EN language toggle in footer

| Tab | Description |

## 3. The Page Builder Tab

This is the most powerful feature of the CMS. It gives you full control over the structure and content of your pages.

### A. Editing a Page

First, select the page you wish to edit from the page tabs (e.g., Home, Adopt, About).

### B. Managing Sections

The page is built from a series of sections. You have full control over them:

-   **Reorder Sections:** Simply click and hold the drag handle (`::`) on the left of any section and drag it up or down to a new position.
-   **Edit Content & Layout:** Click the expand arrow (`‚ñæ`) on a section to open the editor. Here you can change text, upload photos, and modify the layout.
-   **Hide a Section:** Click the eye icon (`üëÅÔ∏è`) to temporarily hide a section from the live site without deleting it.
-   **Duplicate a Section:** Click the copy icon (`‚ùê`) to create an exact copy of a section, which you can then modify.
-   **Delete a Section:** Click the trash can icon (`üóëÔ∏è`) to permanently remove a section.
-   **Add a New Section:** Click the `+ Add Section` button at the bottom to choose from 11 pre-designed layout templates.

### C. Editing Section Content

Inside a section's editor, you can modify:

-   **Layout:** Choose a different visual style for the section.
-   **Content:** Type directly into the `Headline`, `Subheadline`, and `Body text` fields.
-   **Buttons / CTAs:** Edit the text and URL for any buttons in the section.
-   **Photos:** Upload images by dragging them into the photo area, clicking to browse, or pasting an image URL.
-   **Colors:** Use the color pickers to change the background, text, and accent colors for that specific section.

### D. Saving

Once you are happy with your changes, click the **`Save Layout`** button at the top right to make them live.

## 4. The Animals Manager Tab

This tab is where you manage all the animal profiles for the adoption pages.

-   **Add an Animal:** Click the `+ Add Animal` button to open a form where you can enter the animal's details (name, species, breed, age, description, etc.) and upload their photo.
-   **Edit an Animal:** Click the `Edit` button on any animal's card to update its information.
-   **Search and Filter:** Use the search bar to find an animal by name, or use the dropdowns to filter by species or status (e.g., show only 
`Available` dogs).

## 5. The Site Settings Tab

This area controls the global settings for your entire website.

-   **Identity:** Set your site name, tagline, and upload your official logo and favicon.
-   **Contact:** Update your shelter‚Äôs physical address, phone number, email, and operating hours.
-   **Social:** Add links to your social media profiles (Facebook, Instagram, etc.).
-   **Colors:** Define your primary, secondary, and accent brand colors that will be used as defaults across the site.
-   **Navigation:** Add, remove, or reorder the links in your main website navigation menu.
-   **Footer:** Edit the content displayed in the website‚Äôs footer.

After making changes in any of these sub-panels, be sure to click the **`Save Settings`** button.
